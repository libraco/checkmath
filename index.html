Introduction & How it works

Address-poisoning scams and clipboard hijackers are rising. Compare every character to make sure you're sending to the right place.

  1. Paste original address: Copy the address from the official source (website, confirmation email, QR code...).
  2. Paste clipboard address: Paste the address you copied from your clipboard - the one you will use to send funds.
  3. Read the result: The tool compares every character and warns you instantly if a scam is detected.

Security Commitment & Tips
  • 100% Browser-Side: All computation runs in your browser. No data ever leaves your device.
  • No Data Storage: No database, no server. Your wallet addresses are never saved or logged.
  • Always verify manually: Never skip checking the full address, especially the middle characters.
  • Limit clipboard access: Use hardware wallets or clipboard-protection browser extensions.
  • Send a test transaction: Always send a tiny amount first to confirm the address before the full amount.
